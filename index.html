Why EDC?

CONNECT

At EDC, we regularly conduct events and workshops and bring in the finest people from the entrepreneurship world. All this is not just to learn, but to connect establish connections with them as well as other like-minded people so that it turns out to be a fruitful lifelong learning.

IMPROVE

Not everyone is born with perfection, but people do reach near it owing to their hard work and continual efforts. EDC encourages you to make mistakes, learn from them and then wear them like badges. The scope for improvement is always high.

LEARN

Learning is a continual process. One who ceases to learn ceases to grow. We bring with us people who possess a great and varied skill set as well experience to inspire and guide you and help you learn skills and also learn from their experiences. At EDC, we never limit ourselves to learning.

DO

This is the most important part. Action is the most important part of any teaching. EDC is the right place for this. We do it ourselves, we make mistakes, we fail, we learn, we do it again. You want to do something, come join us!
